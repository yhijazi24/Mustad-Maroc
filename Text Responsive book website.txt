========== About Us ==========
.about__title
About Us

.about__description
Mustad Hoofcare, a trusted name since 1832, is a leading provider of innovative solutions in equine hoof care. Specializing in horseshoes, nails, and advanced hoof care supplements, Mustad Hoofcare is dedicated to promoting the health and performance of horses worldwide. Their commitment to blending tradition with cutting-edge technology makes them a go-to choice for horse owners, farriers, and equine professionals seeking reliable solutions for optimal hoof health.
