MUSTAD HAMMERHEAD NAIL

Safely lock into the race shoe's crease

The HammerHead nail is specifically designed to fit all race plates. The distinct neck and head waffle design, combined with the exclusive Mustad Pitch technology, provides a safe locking solution into the shoe crease. A long and slim shank, to drive high when needed, provides an easy and strong clinching. The sharp, exact point ensures a consistent drive, minimizing the hoof wall impact to an absolute minimum.
